Easy Christmas Sugar Cookie Recipe by Umami Girl: üç™

You will need:

  • 227g Unsalted Butter (Room Temperature)
  • 112g Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 300g All-Purpose Flour

    • Intructions:

  • 1. Preheat oven to 325¬∞F with a rack in the center
  • 2. Cut the butter into small pieces and place it in a large bowl with the sugar. Beat until light in color and fluffy. Then, add the Vanilla Extract and beat again.
  • 3. Add the flour and stir with spatula or wooden spoon until combined (you can also mix with hands when mostly combined). Do not overmix!
  • 4. Dust work surface and rolling pin with flour. Roll the dough into a 1/4-inch thick circle. Cut shapes with a cookie cutter! Tip: Re-roll scraps to make more cookies
  • 5. Transfer cookies to a parchment paper lined cookie sheet. Chill in fridge for 15 minutes.
  • 6. Bake for 15 minutes!
  • 7. Remove from oven and let cookies cool completely
  • 8. Decorate!
